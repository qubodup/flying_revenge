"Flying Revenge: Kill The Humans" made 2010 by Iwan "qubodup" Gabovitch <qubodup@gmail.com> <qubodup.net>

Made while and after First Jam! of Berlin Game Developers Meet-Up in Berlin 2010 <http://www.facebook.com/group.php?gid=158191674197213&v=app_2344061033>

Shitty idea by me via brain.
Code by me via vim.
Graphics by me via GIMP.
Sounds by me via EeePC 1000H mic + Audacity.

Everything licensed under BSD, MIT and zLib (pick one).

You need LÖVE <http://love2d.org/> to play.

$ cd nameofthedirectory
$ love .

Or drag-and-drop on a LÖVE executable.
